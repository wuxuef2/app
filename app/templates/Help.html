How to use it

When you upload the image, application will find a child face on it and mark facial points. But sometimes application couldn't mark well, you need to move points to proper place. When you upload the image, application will give a Estimated age. But sometimes application maybe make a misstake, just modify it. After that, set the old age, and click Aging button. Enjoy it!
